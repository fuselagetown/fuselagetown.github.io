What do I do?

  • Strategy and innovation
    • Triangulate qual and quant research to identify and frame client problems
    • Define which client-centric solutions to pursue with the highest potential business impact
    • Break the problem down and prioritize for design and other functions (eg. copy, creative curation, engineering, data science)
    • Identify where teams can push themselves to truly explore what’s possible
    • Facilitate cross-functional workshop (eg design sprints, roadmap planning)
  • Delivery and execution
    • Deliver high results that drive business value and industry recognition
    • Execute extremely fast
    • Judge what's “just enough fidelity” deliverables
    • Pioneer innovative research approaches with realistic prototypes with live, personalized data
  • Communication and influence
    • Communicate well in writing, my design documents and research summaries travel far and wide in the organization
    • Present condfidently to any team size; All hands, design critiques, C-suite etc
    • Build strong relationships across the organisation to help achieve cross-functional alignment
    • Get industry recognition from speaking and being interviewed in the media
    • Recruit top talent / key member of the team hiring panel
  • I am also
    • Amiable and a good team player
    • Tool agnostic; Currently I use google docs, Figma and Jekyll for prototyping
    • Experienced in working in agile environments / pods
    • Endlessly enthusiastic about new products and apps
