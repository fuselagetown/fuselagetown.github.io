Strategy and innovation

  • • Triangulating qual and quant research to identify and frame client problems
  • • Defining which client-centric solutions to pursue with the highest potential business impact
  • • Breaking the problem down and prioritizing for design and other functions (eg. copy, creative curation, engineering, data science)
  • • Identifying where teams can push themselves to truly explore what’s possible
  • • Strong cross-functional workshop facilitator (eg design sprints, roadmap planning)

Delivery and execution

  • • Delivering high results that drive business value and industry recognition
